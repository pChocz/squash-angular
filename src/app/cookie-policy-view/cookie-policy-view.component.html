Cookie Policy


  • We use cookies in order to keep you logged in, otherwise you would need to type your password on each request.
  • Of course it doesn't mean that we store your credentials. Only the JWT (token) is stored within the app and it's not possible to extract credentials from it.
  • We also keep 'confirm-cookie-consent' cookies (so called cookie-ception), to make sure that the cookie consent will not show up again after accepting the policy.


The end of cookie policy. I'm glad that you spent your time reading it. Enjoy using the app!
